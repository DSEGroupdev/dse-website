Investment

How pricing works.

  • One-time setup. Building the agent: your scripts, your tone, your escalation rules, your integrations. Fixed price, agreed up front.
  • Monthly fee. Phone infrastructure, model costs, monitoring, and continuous tuning as we listen to real calls.
  • Usage scales with volume. An office taking 20 calls a day pays less than a call center taking 2,000. You only pay for minutes used.
  • Compare it to the alternative. Price it against a missed-call rate or an answering service, not against software. That is the math that matters.
Questions

What owners ask before they commit.

Will callers know it is AI?

Yes, if you want them to, and we recommend it. Callers care most that they get an instant answer and a resolved request. Modern agents deliver both, and honesty builds trust.

What if it cannot handle a call?

It hands off, following your rules: transfer to a person, take a structured message, or book a callback. The agent never improvises outside the escalation paths you define.

Does it work with my number and CRM?

Yes. Agents work with your existing phone number and connect to common CRMs, calendars, and booking tools, so every call ends with the work already done.

Can it connect to a digital brain?

Yes, as an optional add-on. If you also run a DSE AI Operating System, call transcripts and outcomes feed into your company memory automatically. Each works fully on its own.
