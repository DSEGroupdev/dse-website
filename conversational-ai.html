What makes ours different

Personality is engineering, not a prompt.

  • Persistent memory. The system remembers each person across sessions and channels, so conversations build instead of resetting.
  • Brand voice, enforced. Tone, vocabulary, and boundaries are designed and tested, so your assistant sounds like you on message one thousand, not just message one.
  • Connected actions. Look up orders, book time, process requests. Conversation is the interface; your systems do the work behind it.
  • Guardrails by default. Clear escalation rules, content boundaries, and human oversight built into every deployment.

Questions

Asked before nearly every build.

Is this just a chatbot?

Old chatbots follow decision trees and break outside them. This holds open-ended conversations, pulls from your real documentation, remembers each person, and takes action in your systems.

Which channels does it run on?

Website chat, SMS, WhatsApp, Instagram and other social DMs, and in-app messaging. One system and one memory across every channel you enable.

How do you keep it on-brand and safe?

Personality, vocabulary, and boundaries are engineered and tested, not just prompted. Every deployment ships with escalation rules, content guardrails, and human oversight.

Can it connect to a digital brain?

Yes, as an optional add-on. If you also run a DSE AI Operating System, conversations feed your company memory. Each product works fully on its own.
